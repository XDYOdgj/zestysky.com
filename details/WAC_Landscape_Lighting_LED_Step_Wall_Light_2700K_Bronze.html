WAC LED Step Light: Modern Indoor and Outdoor Wall Light

Elevate your space with the sleek and stylish WAC Landscape Lighting step and wall lights. Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, these lights offer a perfect blend of functionality and aesthetics, seamlessly integrating into your home or garden decor. With a built-in LED bulb, you get energy-efficient illumination that delivers brilliant brightness without any harsh glare.

These lights are engineered to endure the elements, thanks to their IP66 waterproof rating, which ensures durability even in harsh weather conditions. Whether you're lighting up a walkway, staircase, or wall, these lights are built to last and perform flawlessly.

Choose from a variety of finishes, including bronze, stainless steel, and brass, to match your preferred style. The versatile design makes them especially suitable for outdoor environments, including coastal areas where corrosion resistance is essential.

Enhance your lighting experience with the smooth dimming feature that allows you to adjust brightness levels from 100% to 10%. This is compatible with a magnetic low-voltage dimmer, giving you complete control over ambiance. For added convenience, an optional photocell sensor ensures automatic on/off functionality based on ambient light conditions.

The turtle-friendly amber color temperature is an extra special feature, making these lights ideal for turtle habitats. This thoughtful detail ensures that the lighting is not only beautiful but also safe and beneficial for wildlife.
