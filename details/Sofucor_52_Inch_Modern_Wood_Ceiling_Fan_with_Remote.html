Sofucor 52-inch Modern Wood Ceiling Fan

Upgrade your space with the Sofucor 52-inch modern wood ceiling fan. This elegant and functional addition is designed to complement farmhouse, modern, or minimalist interiors perfectly. The low profile flush mount ensures a sleek fit, ideal for both indoor and outdoor environments, from cozy bedrooms to open patios.

Equipped with a powerful DC motor, this fan delivers strong airflow while operating quietly, making it an excellent choice for a peaceful living environment. The reversible motor allows you to switch between cooling and ventilation modes, adapting to seasonal needs. Enjoy the convenience of remote control capabilities that offer seamless adjustment of speed, direction, and timers for 15, 45, or 85 minutes.

Featuring three durable wood blades and rustproof downrods, this fan is built for long-lasting use. The adjustable downrods (5", 10", 24") make it versatile for different ceiling heights and room styles. Whether you want to add a rustic touch to your home or create a modern look in your commercial space, this fan provides both style and practicality for everyday comfort.
