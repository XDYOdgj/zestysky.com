Durable RGB LED Strip Connectors for Professional Lighting Projects

BTF-LIGHTING's connector kit is designed with precision and ease in mind, making it an essential tool for anyone working with RGB and RGBW LED strips. The transparent connectors allow full light exposure and maintain the visual integrity of your setup without any obstruction. Whether you're creating a custom smart lighting display or setting up a projector, this kit ensures a seamless and efficient installation process.

With a low-profile design, these connectors minimize aesthetic disruption, providing a clean and professional look. The set includes 8 strip-to-strip connectors, 8 pairs of corner connectors, and 8 extension wires of 15cm, giving you all the tools needed to extend, connect, or splice your LED strips effortlessly. No soldering is required, making setup and use quick and hassle-free.

Key Features

  • Supports RGB LED strips with 3-pin FCOB SPI WS2811/WS2814 connections
  • Transparent connectors for uninterrupted light output and seamless installation
  • 8 pairs of corner connectors for precise and clean corner splicing
  • Includes 8pcs 15cm extension wires for easy lengthening of LED strips
  • No soldering required - simple plug-and-play operation for quick setup

Why Choose This Kit?

This connector kit is ideal for both DIY enthusiasts and professional installers due to its firm fixing mechanism and tinny construction. It ensures secure and durable connections without adding bulk, and every connector is disposable for easy use and removal. Perfect for creating eye-catching lighting installations or enhancing your smart lighting system.
