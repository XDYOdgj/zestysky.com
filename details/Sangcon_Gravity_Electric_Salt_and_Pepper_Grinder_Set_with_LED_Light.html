Sangcon Gravity Electric Salt and Pepper Grinder Set with LED Light

Transform your kitchen routine with the Sangcon Gravity Electric Salt and Pepper Grinder Set. Designed for both everyday use and special occasions, this modern and functional tool brings convenience to your spice management.

Each grinder in the set is equipped with a generous capacity: 184 grams of sea salt and 100 grams of white pepper. This ensures you have a steady supply of premium spices without frequent refills. The clear BPA-free acrylic body allows for easy visibility of the contents, making it simple to check what's inside at a glance.

With gravity sensing technology, the set eliminates the need for manual grinding or button presses. Simply flip it, and the grinding mechanism activates automatically, offering a seamless one-handed experience. The soft blue LED light provides gentle illumination over the grinding area, making it easy to adjust the seasoning even in low light.

The adjustable ceramic grinding knob lets you choose the perfect coarseness for your dish, whether you're cooking a simple meal or preparing a gourmet feast. Clean up is a breeze thanks to the separate motor and spout design, which ensures easy disassembly and maintenance.

The Sangcon Gravity Electric Salt and Pepper Grinder Set is a great gift for Mother's Day, Father's Day, or Christmas. It combines modern design with practicality, and is built to last with its durable construction and battery-powered operation.
