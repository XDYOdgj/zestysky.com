Enhance Your Outdoor Space with the WAC Bronze LED Step Light

Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, this WAC LED step and wall light combines durability with modern design. Its bronze finish is not only visually appealing but also built to withstand harsh weather conditions, making it an excellent choice for coastal, marine, or any challenging environment.

Superior Illumination

Powered by high-performance LED technology, this step light delivers bright illumination with a luminous flux of up to 68Lm. Whether you're lighting your stairs, walkways, or walls, it ensures clear visibility without excessive glare.

Weather Resistance

With an IP66 waterproof rating, this outdoor wall light is protected against dust, rain, and water jets, ensuring long-lasting performance in any weather. Perfect for use around pools, patios, or garden paths.

Eco-Friendly & Smart

Enjoy energy efficiency with LED bulbs that last up to 60,000 hours. The built-in LED setup removes the need for separate bulbs, and the automatic photocell sensor ensures lights turn on at dusk and off at dawn, offering convenience and reduced energy consumption.

Stylish Finish Options

This light is available in a range of finishes, including bronze, stainless steel, and solid brass, allowing you to match it with your outdoor decor or choose a classic, rustic look. Its elegant design adds both beauty and functionality to any setting.

Color Flexibility & Wildlife-Friendly Glow

Choose from warm white and amber color temperatures to suit your interior or exterior lighting needs. The amber option is especially turtle-friendly, ensuring your lighting is both beautiful and environmentally conscious.

Smooth Dimming Control

This WAC LED step light features smooth dimming from 100% to 10%, offering customizable brightness levels. Paired with a magnetic low voltage dimmer, it gives you complete control over the ambiance and lighting intensity of your area.

Whether you're upgrading your home or enhancing your commercial property, the WAC Bronze LED Step Light provides a blend of practicality, style, and energy efficiency. It's the perfect choice for those who want reliable and beautiful lighting solutions.
