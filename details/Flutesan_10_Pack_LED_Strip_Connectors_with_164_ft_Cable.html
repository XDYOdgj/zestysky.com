Flutesan 10-Pack LED Strip Connectors

Upgrade your lighting system with Flutesan's 10-pack LED strip connectors. These premium connectors are designed to make your lighting projects easier, faster, and more stylish. With their transparent, rugged, and safe build, they seamlessly blend with any LED strip setup, adding a modern touch to your space.

Each pack comes with a 16.4 ft extension cable, allowing you to extend your light coverage effortlessly without extra hassle. Whether you're working on home decor, workshop lighting, or a commercial project, these connectors are built to meet your needs with reliability and precision.

Flutesan connectors are compatible with a wide range of lighting systems including 5V, 12V, and 24V LED strips. They also work with 12mm and 22 AWG 5 conductor wires, ensuring flexibility for different applications. No soldering is required, making installation simple and quick for both beginners and experts.

The durable tin-plated copper wiring offers long-lasting performance and sturdy connections. These connectors are ideal for track lighting, speaker wiring, and other DIY or professional lighting installations, offering a seamless and secure way to connect your systems without the need for complicated tools.

Perfect for lighting system accessory needs, these connectors are a must-have for anyone looking to enhance their lighting with ease and efficiency.
